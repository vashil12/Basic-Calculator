This is the info section and it will provide information about how to use the calculator. Assume you need to do the following calculation: 10 + 25

Step 1: insert the first number of your operation in the first input. i.e 10

Step 2: Choose the operation that you want to do from the list. for this scenario it will be +

Step 3: insert the second number of your operation in the second input. i.e 25

Step 4: Press the calculate button to view the result

just after clicking the calculate button, the full operation and the result will be displayed. you need to press the back button to perform another calculation
